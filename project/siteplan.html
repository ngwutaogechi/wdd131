Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is Bay Area eBiking Club. This name represents a club focused on electric biking in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Optional domain availability: bay-ebike.org

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: What is the best e-bike to get for mountainous areas?

Purpose: This question will guide the content related to the best e-bike options suitable for mountainous terrains, considering factors like battery life, motor power, and durability.

Scenario 2

Question: Where can I find contact information for the club's directors?

Purpose: This question will ensure that the site includes a clear and easily accessible section for contact information, especially for reaching out to the club's directors or other key members.

Scenario 3

Question: Are there any upcoming e-bike events or meetups in the Bay Area?

Purpose: This question will drive the inclusion of an events calendar or announcement section where members can stay informed about upcoming events, rides, and meetups.

Scenario 4

Question: Can I join the club if I'm new to e-biking?

Purpose: This question will highlight the club's inclusivity and provide information for beginners, including how to join, membership benefits, and beginner-friendly resources.

Color Schema

Primary Color: Blue (#1E90FF)

Used for headings, navigation bar, and call-to-action buttons to create a trustworthy and reliable feel.

Secondary Color: Green (#32CD32)

Used for hyperlinks, icons, and borders to add a touch of nature and energy to the site.
